MY MANIFESTO FOR A BETTER DELHI HIGH COURT BAR ASSOCIATION

  • 1. Reimagining the Digital Gateway to Justice
    • Intuitive Digital Platform: A revamped website/app that simplifies application processes, outlines case types, and automates fee calculations.
    • Seamless Functionality: Enhanced search tools, real-time updates, and a comprehensive digital library of legal resources.
  • 2. A Greener High Court for a Sustainable Future
    • Eco-Friendly Infrastructure: Upgrading court premises with solar panels, LED lighting, and energy-efficient appliances.
    • Lush Green Spaces: Expanding green areas with native flora to create a serene, eco-conscious environment.
    • Smart Waste Management: Implementing recyclable and biodegradable solutions for waste management.
  • 3. Uninterrupted Connectivity & Speed
    • Upgraded Wi-Fi: Enhancing internet connectivity with modern routers and increased bandwidth for efficient legal research and virtual hearings.
  • 4. Accessible Drinking Water – Hydration as a Right
    • Strategic Water Points: Installing additional water dispensers for easy access.
    • Hygienic Convenience: Providing disposable paper cups for a safe and clean refreshment experience.
  • 5. Streamlined Printing Services
    • More Printing Machines: Increasing printer availability in high-demand areas to reduce wait times.
    • Enhanced Accessibility: Ensuring quick and reliable printing of essential documents.
  • 6. Always Charged – Court-Wide USB-C Charging
    • Modern Charging Stations: Installing dedicated USB-C hubs to keep devices powered and ready.
  • 7. Future-Ready with Electric Vehicle Charging
    • Eco-Infrastructure: Setting up charging stations for electric vehicles, with renewable energy integration.
  • 8. Transparent Appointments for Local Commissioners
    • Clear Guidelines: Introducing standardized application forms and transparent criteria for appointments.
    • Empowering Young Professionals: Creating fair opportunities to bolster trust and encourage new talent.
  • 9. Delhi High Court Help Desk – Your Information Hub
    • Comprehensive Support: Establishing a dedicated help desk for guidance on court procedures and digital navigation.
    • Responsive Service: Ensuring every query is met with knowledgeable and courteous assistance.
  • 10. Onsite Pharmacy Services for Wellness
    • Accessible Healthcare: Launching a dedicated pharmacy for prescriptions, OTC medications, and professional advice.
    • Health-Centric Environment: Providing first-aid supplies and wellness products for court users.
