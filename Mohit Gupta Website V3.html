MY MANIFESTO FOR A BETTER DELHI HIGH COURT BAR ASSOCIATION

  • 1. Reimagining the Digital Gateway to Justice
    • Intuitive Digital Platform: A completely revamped website/app that simplifies finding the right application, clearly outlines case types, and automates fee calculations.
    • Seamless Functionality: Reliable search tools across devices, real-time display board updates, and an extensive digital library for bare acts and legal resources.
  • 2. A Greener High Court for a Sustainable Future
    • Eco-Friendly Infrastructure: Upgrade court premises with solar panels, LED lighting, and energy-efficient appliances.
    • Lush Green Spaces: Expand green areas with native flora to create serene, eco-conscious environments that counter urban heat.
    • Smart Waste Management: Implement recyclable and biodegradable packaging along with clear waste segregation practices.
  • 3. Uninterrupted Connectivity & Speed
    • Upgraded Wi-Fi: Enhance the Court’s internet by upgrading routers and increasing bandwidth to ensure faster legal research, smoother virtual hearings, and efficient access to documents.
  • 4. Accessible Drinking Water – Hydration as a Right
    • Strategic Water Points: Install additional water dispensers in key locations.
    • Hygienic Convenience: Provide disposable paper cups to ensure clean and accessible refreshment for all.
  • 5. Streamlined Printing Services
    • More Printing Machines: Increase the number of printers in high-demand areas to reduce waiting times and improve workflow efficiency.
    • Enhanced Accessibility: Ensure that essential documents—from judgments to reference materials—are printed quickly and reliably.
  • 6. Always Charged – Court-Wide USB-C Charging
    • Modern Charging Stations: Install dedicated USB-C charging hubs across the court to keep your devices powered, ensuring that connectivity and productivity never suffer.
  • 7. Future-Ready with Electric Vehicle Charging
    • Eco-Infrastructure: Set up dedicated charging stations for e-cars and e-bikes.
    • Renewable Integration: Explore solar-powered options to support a cleaner, sustainable future.
  • 8. Transparent Appointments for Local Commissioners
    • Clear Guidelines: Introduce standardized application forms, public vacancy notices, and transparent criteria for Local Commissioner appointments.
    • Empowering Young Professionals: Create fair opportunities that bolster trust and encourage new talent in our legal community.
  • 9. Delhi High Court Help Desk – Your Information Hub
    • Comprehensive Support: Establish a dedicated help desk to provide guidance on court procedures, case updates, document filing, and digital navigation.
    • Responsive Service: Ensure every query is met with knowledgeable and courteous assistance.
  • 10. Onsite Pharmacy Services for Wellness
    • Accessible Healthcare: Launch a dedicated pharmacy offering prescription dispensing, over-the-counter medications, and professional pharmaceutical advice.
    • Health-Centric Environment: Provide first-aid supplies and wellness products to support the well-being of all court users.
