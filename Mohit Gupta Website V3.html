TRANSFORMING THE DELHI HIGH COURT

A VISION FOR MODERN JUSTICE, SUSTAINABILITY & TRANSPARENCY

Me, in a brief...

I am Mohit Gupta—a dedicated legal professional who believes in harnessing technology and sustainable practices to make justice more accessible and efficient. With years of experience in the legal field, I have witnessed firsthand the challenges our community faces. I stand for transparency, innovation, and environmental responsibility. My mission is to modernize the Delhi High Court so that every stakeholder—from seasoned lawyers to first-time litigants—experiences a seamless, fair, and supportive judicial system.

MY MANIFESTO FOR A BETTER DELHI HIGH COURT BAR ASSOCIATION

1. REIMAGINING THE DIGITAL GATEWAY TO JUSTICE: A completely revamped website/app that simplifies finding the right application, clearly outlines case types, and automates fee calculations. This also includes reliable search tools across devices, real-time display board updates, and an extensive digital library for bare acts and legal resources.

2. A GREENER HIGH COURT FOR A SUSTAINABLE FUTURE: Upgrade court premises with solar panels, LED lighting, and energy-efficient appliances. Expand green areas with native flora to create serene, eco-conscious environments that counter urban heat, and implement recyclable and biodegradable packaging with clear waste segregation practices.

3. UNINTERRUPTED CONNECTIVITY & SPEED: Enhance the Court’s internet by upgrading routers and increasing bandwidth to ensure faster legal research, smoother virtual hearings, and efficient access to documents.

4. ACCESSIBLE DRINKING WATER – HYDRATION AS A RIGHT: Install additional water dispensers in key locations and provide disposable paper cups to ensure clean and accessible refreshment for all.

5. STREAMLINED PRINTING SERVICES: Increase the number of printers in high-demand areas to reduce waiting times and improve workflow efficiency, ensuring that essential documents—from judgments to reference materials—are printed quickly and reliably.

6. ALWAYS CHARGED – COURT-WIDE USB-C CHARGING: Install dedicated USB-C charging hubs across the court to keep your devices powered, ensuring that connectivity and productivity never suffer.

7. FUTURE-READY WITH ELECTRIC VEHICLE CHARGING: Set up dedicated charging stations for e-cars and e-bikes, and explore solar-powered options to support a cleaner, sustainable future.

8. TRANSPARENT APPOINTMENTS FOR LOCAL COMMISSIONERS: Introduce standardized application forms, public vacancy notices, and transparent criteria for Local Commissioner appointments while creating fair opportunities that bolster trust and encourage new talent in the legal community.

9. DELHI HIGH COURT HELP DESK – YOUR INFORMATION HUB: Establish a dedicated help desk to provide guidance on court procedures, case updates, document filing, and digital navigation. Ensure every query is met with knowledgeable and courteous assistance.

10. ONSITE PHARMACY SERVICES FOR WELLNESS: Launch a dedicated pharmacy offering prescription dispensing, over-the-counter medications, and professional pharmaceutical advice, along with first-aid supplies and wellness products to support the well-being of all court users.
